Part 1: Sarf Warm-up
10 minutes · اللفيف المقرون + المضاعف
▼

This week introduces two difficult classes: اللفيف المقرون and المضاعف. You will practice وَقَى يَقِي and مَدَّ يَمُدُّ, then apply them in the section on المجمل والبيان.

💡 لفيف مقرون — وَقَى يَقِي Two weak radicals combine rules: initial weak behavior plus final weak deletion in jussive/imperative.

Week 7 Key Takeaways

I'rab mastery targets
  • Identify iḍāfah chains quickly: noun + noun(genitive).
  • Separate semantic iḍāfah (معنوية) from formal iḍāfah (لفظية).
  • Track adjective agreement inside iḍāfah phrases.
Sarf mastery targets
  • لفيف verbs combine weak-initial and weak-final behavior.
  • مضاعف verbs require attention to where shaddah opens or resolves.
  • Quick recognition improves parsing speed in dense prose.
