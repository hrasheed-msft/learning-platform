Part 4: Grammar Connection — Qatr al-Nada
25 minutes · إنّ وأخواتها
▼

Connect usul scope statements to inna-family syntax.

قطر الندى — إنّ وأخواتها
إن وأخواتها حروف ناسخة تنصب الاسم وترفع الخبر نحو إن العام واسع ولكن الخاص معتبر ولعل البيان قريب
Translation “Inna and its sisters are particles that set the noun accusative and raise the predicate.”
ConceptArabicWeek 5 Example
اسم إنّالْعَامَّIn إن العام شامل, noun of inna is منصوب.
خبر إنّشَامِلٌPredicate remains marfūʿ.
لكنّلَكِنَّ الْخَاصَّSame rule: noun accusative.
لعلّلَعَلَّ الْبَيَانَExpectation particle from same family.
📌 The big insight Week 5 asks dual discrimination: syntactic reversers (إنّ-family vs كان-family) and hollow-verb class differences in Sarf.

Week 5 Key Takeaways

I'rab patterns to internalise
  • Distinguish اسم إنّ from اسم كان instantly.
  • Watch scope operators to control legal meaning in عام/خاص arguments.
  • Track passive reshaping in specification clauses.
Sarf patterns to internalise
  • Hollow wāwī and yāʾī diverge in imperfect and jussive vowels.
  • Imperatives preserve that contrast: صُمْ vs بِعْ.
  • Use these cues to infer weak roots in unvowelled prose.
