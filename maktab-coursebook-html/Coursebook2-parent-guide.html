Wuḍū'

📝 Lesson Summary

Whenever a person wants to meet someone special, they make sure that they get ready properly. When we pray our ṣalāh, we are going to speak to Allāh , the King of all kings. Wuḍū' is preparing us for ṣalāh by cleaning our body. Also by doing wuḍū', sins that we might have done are washed away! Ṣalāh cannot be performed without wuḍū'.

Key Points:

  • When we pray our ṣalāh, we are going to speak to Allāh , the King of all kings.
  • Wuḍū' is preparing us for ṣalāh by cleaning our body.
  • Also by doing wuḍū', sins that we might have done are washed away! Ṣalāh cannot be performed without wuḍū'.
  • The Qur'ān cannot be touched without wuḍū', although it is permissible to recite from memory without wuḍū'.
  • Wuḍū' is a type of worship which includes washing certain parts of the body.

  • Some are far}, some are sunnah whilst others are mustaḥabb.
  • There are also some which are makrūh and others which are nāqi}.
  • Far} is something that you have to do.
