Week 8 Key Takeaways

I'rab mastery targets
  • Capstone standard: justify every ending by its عامل.
  • Treat iḍāfah and prepositional chains as structural anchors.
  • Explain alternatives when multiple parses are possible.
Sarf mastery targets
  • Rapid category recognition is now mandatory, not optional.
  • Always state root + pattern + observed transformation.
  • Final mastery: parse unvowelled text with independent reasoning.
